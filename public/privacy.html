Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 11, 2025

Welcome to CV Cloud. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web services.

By using CV Cloud, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create a CV using CV Cloud, we collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide:

Data Type Purpose Required
Name CV content and account identification Yes
Email Address Account management, authentication, and contact Yes
Phone Number CV contact information Yes
Physical Address CV contact information Optional
Date of Birth CV personal information Optional
Work History CV employment section Optional
Education Details CV education section Optional
Skills & Languages CV skills section Optional

1.2 Photos and Videos

  • Profile Photos: Professional headshots for your CV (optional)
  • Video Introductions: Short video presentations (optional)

1.3 Files and Documents

  • Certificates: Educational or professional certifications (optional)
  • Supporting Documents: Any additional documents you choose to attach (optional)

1.4 User-Generated Content

All content you create for your CV, including:

  • Personal summaries
  • Work experience descriptions
  • References
  • Interests and attributes
  • Any other text or content you input

1.5 Account Information

  • User ID: Automatically generated unique identifier
  • Password: Encrypted and securely stored
  • Account Settings: Your preferences and configurations

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 App Functionality

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your professional CV
  • Enable you to edit, update, and manage your CV content
  • Allow you to choose from multiple CV templates
  • Generate CV previews and exports
  • Store your CV data securely in the cloud

2.2 Account Management

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Authenticate your identity when you log in
  • Provide password recovery and account support
  • Sync your data across devices

2.3 Communication

  • Send important service notifications
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Notify you about security updates
  • Inform you about new features (if you opt-in)

2.4 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Monitor for security threats
  • Verify user identity for account recovery

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Encryption

Security Measures:
  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption
  • Data is stored on secure cloud servers
  • Regular security audits and updates

3.2 Data Retention

  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account and data at any time
  • Upon account deletion, most data is removed immediately
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (up to 90 days in backups)

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data

Important: CV Cloud does NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our app:

  • Cloud Hosting: For data storage and app hosting
  • Email Services: For sending authentication and notification emails
  • Analytics: For app performance monitoring (anonymized data only)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

4.3 User-Initiated Sharing

You control when and how your CV is shared:

  • You can export your CV as a PDF
  • You can email your CV directly from the app
  • You can choose to make your CV publicly viewable (opt-in)
  • You can generate shareable links to your CV

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal processes (subpoenas, court orders)
  • Government or regulatory requests
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigation of fraud or security issues

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your personal data stored in the app
  • Edit: Update or correct your information at any time
  • Export: Download your CV and data
  • Delete: Remove specific data or delete your entire account

5.2 Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  • Open the CV Cloud app
  • Go to Settings → Menu → "Delete my account"
  • Or visit: www.cvcloud.app/data-deletion
  • Or email us at: support@cvcloud.app

5.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time by:

  • Clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email
  • Adjusting your notification preferences in Settings
  • Contacting us at support@cvcloud.app
