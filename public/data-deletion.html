Account & Data Deletion

CV Cloud - Professional CV Builder

We respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your CV Cloud account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Delete Account In-App (Recommended)

  1. Open the CV Cloud mobile app
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Tap the menu icon (three horizontal lines) in the top right corner
  4. Scroll down in the menu
  5. Tap "Delete my account"
  6. Confirm your decision when prompted
  7. Your account and data will be permanently deleted

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to contact us directly:

  1. Send an email to: support@cvcloud.app
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your CV Cloud account, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, address
  • CV Content: All CV information, work history, education, skills, languages
  • Profile Photos: Any uploaded profile pictures
  • Video Introductions: Any recorded video introductions
  • Documents: Uploaded certificates and supporting documents
  • Account Data: Login credentials, preferences, settings
  • Shared CVs: Any CVs you've shared or made public

Data Retention & Exceptions

Immediate Deletion: Most of your data is deleted immediately upon account deletion.

Legal Retention: Some data may be retained for a limited period to comply with legal obligations:

  • Transaction records: Retained for 7 years (if applicable for tax/accounting purposes)
  • Legal compliance data: Retained as required by law
  • Backup systems: Data may remain in backup systems for up to 90 days before permanent deletion

What Happens After Deletion

  • You will no longer be able to log in to your account
  • All your CVs and personal information will be permanently removed
  • Any shared CV links will no longer work
  • This action cannot be undone
  • You can create a new account at any time, but previous data will not be recoverable

Alternative: Deactivate Instead of Delete

If you're not sure about permanently deleting your account, you can:

  • Simply stop using the app (your data remains secure and private)
  • Remove app data by uninstalling the app from your device
  • Contact us to temporarily deactivate your account

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or data privacy:

Email: support@cvcloud.app

Privacy Policy: View our Privacy Policy

Response Time: We typically respond within 24-48 hours
